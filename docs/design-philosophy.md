# Design Philosophy

The Logic Lab boards were created to reduce the barrier of entry to FPGA experimentation and digital logic education.

Many FPGA development boards are either:

- Too expensive for students
- Difficult to obtain locally
- Lacking beginner-friendly peripherals
- Overly focused on high-end FPGA applications

The goal of Logic Lab is different.

These boards prioritize:

- Accessibility
- Affordability
- Hands-on experimentation
- Immediate interaction with digital logic
- Educational simplicity
